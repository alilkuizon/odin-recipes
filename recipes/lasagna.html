Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is one of the tastiest dishes anyone can make. You can make it in any occasion

It is not that difficult to create but it is very yummy. Just follow the steps below

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Pork
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, tomato sauce and flavorings
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready.
  3. From there, it's just an assembly job.
  4. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
