Adobo

Description

Adobo is one of the filipino dishes anyone can make. You can make it in any occasion

It is not that difficult to create but it is very yummy. Just follow the steps below

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Onion
  • Bay Leaves
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Combine pork, soy sauce and garlic then marinade for an hour
  2. Heat and cook the marinated pork in the pan
  3. Pour remaining marinade including garlic
  4. Add water, pepper and bay leaves then simmer
  5. Put vinegar and simmer
  6. Put salt to taste and serve hot and enjoy
