Menudo

Description

Menudo is one of the tastiest dishes anyone can make. You can make it in any occasion

It is not that difficult to create but it is very yummy. Just follow the steps below

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Potato
  • Carrots

Steps

  1. Cook pork with carrots and potato
  2. Add tomato sauce and/or ketchup.
  3. Add a bit of water and starch
  4. Heat and simmer
  5. Enjoy
